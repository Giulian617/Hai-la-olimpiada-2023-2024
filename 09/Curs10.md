## Fibonacci

$$
F(n) = \begin{cases} 
0 & \text{if } n = 0 \\
1 & \text{if } n = 1 \\
F(n-1) + F(n-2) & \text{otherwise}
\end{cases}
$$


$$
 * F_{n-1} F_{n+1} - F_n^2 = (-1)^n
 * F_{n+k} = F_k F_{n+1} + F_{k-1} F_n
 * F_{2n} = F_n (F_{n+1} + F_{n-1})
 * GCD(F_m, F_n) = F_{GCD(m, n)}

$$
